# Subject Line Formulas

## High-Converting Formulas

### Curiosity Gap
- "The [topic] secret nobody talks about"
- "What [successful people] know that you don't"
- "I was wrong about [topic]"

### How-To
- "How to [achieve result] in [timeframe]"
- "How [name] went from [A] to [B]"
- "How to [goal] without [pain point]"

### List/Number
- "[Number] ways to [achieve outcome]"
- "[Number] mistakes killing your [metric]"
- "The [number]-step system for [result]"

### Question
- "Are you making this [topic] mistake?"
- "What if [dream scenario]?"
- "Ready to [achieve goal]?"

### Urgency/Scarcity
- "[X hours] left: [offer]"
- "Last chance: [benefit]"
- "Ending tonight: [specific offer]"

### Personal
- "[First name], quick question"
- "I noticed something about your [behavior]"
- "A gift for you, [First name]"

### Social Proof
- "How [company] achieved [result]"
- "[Number] people already [action]"
- "Join [number]+ [audience] who [benefit]"

## Subject Line Best Practices

| Factor | Recommendation |
|--------|----------------|
| Length | 30-50 characters |
| Personalization | Use when relevant |
| Emoji | Test, 1 max |
| Caps | Avoid ALL CAPS |
| Spam words | Avoid "free", "act now" |

## Preview Text (Preheader)

- Extends subject line (don't repeat)
- 40-90 characters
- Include CTA or benefit
- Create curiosity

**Examples:**
- Subject: "Your weekly marketing insights"
- Preview: "Plus a free template to save 5 hours"

## A/B Test Ideas

1. Question vs. statement
2. With vs. without emoji
3. With vs. without numbers
4. Short vs. medium length
5. With vs. without personalization

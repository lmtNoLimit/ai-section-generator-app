```markdown

## Background
[Context and reason for campaign]

## Objective
**Primary Goal:** [Specific, measurable objective]
**Secondary Goals:** [Additional objectives]

## Target Audience
**Primary Segment:**
- Demographics: [Age, location, etc.]
- Psychographics: [Interests, behaviors]
- Pain Points: [Problems we solve]

**Secondary Segment:** [If applicable]

## Key Messages
**Primary Message:**
[Main thing we want audience to remember]

**Supporting Messages:**
1. [Message 1]
2. [Message 2]
3. [Message 3]

### Timeline
- Planning: [dates]
- Asset Creation: [dates]
- Launch: [date]
- Duration: [length]

### Budget

- Paid Media: $[X]
- Creative: $[X]
- Other: $[X]

### Channel Mix
| Channel | Allocation | Role |
|---------|------------|------|
| [Channel] | $[X] / [%] | [Purpose] |

### Assets Needed

|-------|-------|-------|-----|

[Tone, style, visual guidelines]

## Success Metrics

| KPI | Target | Measurement |
|-----|--------|-------------|
| [Metric] | [Value] | [How tracked] |

## Approval
- Brief Approved By: [Name] on [Date]
- Creative Approved By: [Name] on [Date]
- Final Sign-off: [Name] on [Date]
```

## Brief Best Practices

1. Keep it focused (1-2 pages)
2. Include clear success criteria
3. Get stakeholder sign-off
4. Share with all team members
5. Reference throughout campaign
